Mike.
Designer.

I'm a multidisciplinary graphic designer with years of experience creating work that spans the full creative spectrum — from high-energy concert flyers and event graphics to refined brand identities, cannabis packaging, merchandise, and digital experiences.

My work lives at the intersection of bold visual culture and intentional design. Whether I'm designing a poster for a sold-out show, building a brand from the ground up, or crafting labels for a premium dispensary product, I bring the same attention to craft and detail.

I've worked with artists, venues, event companies, brands, dispensaries, and creative agencies — always with the goal of making work that stops people mid-scroll and communicates something real.

Good design isn't just decoration. It builds trust, communicates value, and makes people feel something. That's what I'm here to do.
